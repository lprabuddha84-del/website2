Artisan Craft Heritage

Elevating Sri Lankan Craft Traditions

Lanka Luxe was born from a desire to elevate Sri Lankan weaving traditions onto the global high-fashion stage. Every garment we design is a product of close collaboration with weaver communities in Kandy and batik artisans in Galle.

By utilizing locally sourced natural fibres—such as fine mulberries and biological linens—we protect our ecosystems while keeping century-old handloom techniques thriving.
